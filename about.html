Hey my name is Sam

I love trying to change things in the world in interesting ways, usually by building interactions with computers. I'm a perfectionist.

I think of design as more than just visible appearances. To me, design is how something functions and how it feels to the user, along with its appearance. I enjoy refining the appearance, the functionality, and the way my projects feel until I see nothing left to improve.

I believe very strongly in the individual's rights to privacy. I think government should serve the people, and act in their interest. The people means everyone, not just those with wealth or power.



If you have some code you want me to look at or an idea worth considering, contact me!
